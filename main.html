How to Use FilteriT:

  • Enter a keyword in the input box and click 'Add' to track it.
  • Use the toggle switches to enable a filter.
  • Click 'Show Word List' to view or remove tracked words.

  • *NOTE*If all items on your webpage dissapear/blur(or similar issues) turn off all switches and refresh the webpage
  • *NOTE*Enabling Adblockers or other script injection extensions might also negatviely impact performance on certain sites
